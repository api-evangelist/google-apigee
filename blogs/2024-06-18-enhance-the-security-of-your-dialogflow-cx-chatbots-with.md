Organizations that want to create a chatbot often turn to Dialogflow , Google Cloud’s service for creating conversational agents that can understand and respond to natural language. Available in two editions, many organizations choose Dialogflow CX for its advanced chatbot capabilities. However, integrating a chatbot with your website in the absence of a middleware solution may risk directly injecting sensitive credentials like service account keys into the frontend, or opting for unauthenticated URLs.
