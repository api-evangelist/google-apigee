Apigee API Management allows the Italian Public Administration and its customers, suppliers, and system integrators to achieve full compliance with the new Interoperability Model (source New Interoperability Model - ModI) written and designed by Agency for Digital Italy (AgID). Apigee as ModI platform enabler One of the key aspects of the ModI is promoting the adoption of an API-first approach in order to help deliver maximum accessibility and interoperability across all the actors and services involved. Apigee provides the right foundation for delivering ModI complaints interaction.
